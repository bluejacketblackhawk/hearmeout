Hear Me Out

Select text anywhere. Press a key. Hear it in a real voice.
All of it on your machine — no cloud, no account, no subscription.

Waking the voice up…

macOS asks your okay for two things. Both live in System Settings → Privacy & Security.

Input Monitoring — hear your hotkey in any app
Accessibility — grab the text you select
  1. Select some text — an article, an email, a PDF in your browser.
  2. Press F8
  3. Listen. Press it again (or Esc) to stop.
