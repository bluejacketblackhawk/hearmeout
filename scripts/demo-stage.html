The $139-a-year feature your computer already has

weekend reading · 4 min

Speechify charges $139 a year to read text out loud. NaturalReader wants $119. Both are fine products built on a premise that quietly expired: that a voice worth listening to has to live in someone else's cloud.

The voice they rent you now runs free on the machine you already own. Select anything, press one key, and listen — no cloud, no account, no subscription.

That's the whole pitch. The rest is just details, and the details are open source.
